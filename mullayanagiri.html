MULLAYANAGIRI

Mulayyanagiri is the highest peak in Karnataka, India. Mullayyanagiri is located in the Chandra Dhrona Hill Ranges of the Western Ghats of Chikkamagaluru Taluk.

  • It is one of the highest peak between the Himalayas and the Nilgiris. Mullayanagiri is one of the best trekking places in Karnataka and South India.
  • A very good place for trekking, 300 concrete steps.
  • A very good place for mountain biking.
  • A Shiva temple is also located on the peak. The views from here are spectacular.
