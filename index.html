OUR KITCHEN

Delight Your Tastebuds

The Vintage offers extraordinary meals through breakfast, lunch and dinner.  Using everything from bamboo shoots to mushrooms and special medicinal plants, the traditional food of Malnad is not just about their famous pork, chicken, mutton and fish dishes.

Catering to both non-vegetarians and vegetarians, we highlight heritage cuisine served with more modern Indian dishes.  Our food is made with lots of care and our chef will be more than happy to whip up mouth watering dishes to satisfy your every craving!

Chikmagalur or Chikkamagaluru literally means 'The Town of the Younger Daughter'. ‘Chikkamagalu’ in kannada means ‘Younger Daughter’ and ‘uru’ in kannada means ‘Town’ or ‘place’ It is said to have been given as a dowry to the younger daughter of Rukmangada, the legendary chief of Sakrepatna. The hill stations around Chikmagalur are famous retreats for a relaxing get away, come and explore the land of coffee!
